on april 20 2000 at 1530 eastern daylight time an air tractor at-301 n8899s collided with the ground while maneuvering for a forced landing near colquitt georgia .
any errors in this report will be corrected when the final report has been completed .
the aerial application flight was operated by the commercial pilot under the provisions of title 14 cfr part 137 with no flight plan filed .
according to the pilot when full throttle was applied for take he was only able to generate 63 % of torque .
the flight 's exact departure time from colquitt georgia was not determined .
the airplane sustained substantial damage and the pilot was not injured .
after 1500 feet of ground roll the pilot elected to abort the takeoff .
the accident occurred as the pilot started initial departure and spraying operation .
visual weather conditions prevailed at the time of the accident .
the airplane collided with a fence on the departure end of the runway .
