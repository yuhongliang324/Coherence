on may 21 2000 at 1530 mountain standard time a cessna 182n airplane n92755 was substantially damaged during a forced landing following a loss of engine power near nogales state of sonora in the republic of mexico .
during a forced landing in rough and uneven sandy terrain the nose landing gear collapsed resulting in a loss of control .
the personal flight originated from mulege state of baja california sur mexico at 1230 .
