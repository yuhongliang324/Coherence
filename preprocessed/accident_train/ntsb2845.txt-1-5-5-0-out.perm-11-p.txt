on march 1 1995 about 1119 eastern standard time a beech v35a n8066r registered to richard h. rowland landed hard at the nassau international airport nassau bahamas while on a 14 cfr part 91 personal flight .
the investigation is under the jurisdiction of the government of the bahamas .
visual meteorological conditions prevailed at the time and no flight plan was filed .
any further information pertaining to this accident may be obtained from director of civil aviation bahamas p. o. box n-975 nassau n. p. bahamas phone 809 327-7117 this report is for information purposes only and contains only information released by the bahamian government .
any errors in this report will be corrected when the final report has been completed .
the airplane was substantially damaged and the pilot and three passengers were not injured .
this is preliminary information subject to change and may contain errors .
the flight originated from congo town andros bahamas at an unknown time the same day .
