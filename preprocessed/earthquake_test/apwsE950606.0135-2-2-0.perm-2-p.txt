greece-earthquake|strong quake hits northern greece .
salonica greece ap a strong earthquake shook northern greece early tuesday causing some panic among residents but police said no serious damage to dwellings or injuries were reported .
police said initial reports indicated minor damage to buildings that had already been hit in a 6.6 earthquake april 13 in the same area that devastated villages and slightly injured 16 people .
the strong tremor was preceded by a smaller one of 3.7 four hours earlier which alerted many residents of kozani and surrounding areas to evacuate homes .
the earthquake recorded at 07 36 am 0437 gmt had a preliminary magnitude of 4.8 and rolled through kozani west of this capital of northern greece the geodynamic institute said .
